About VALERIA VILLAS

We are an Architectural Design Company based in Nairobi, Kenya.

We offer Architectural design services ranging from entire design projects, interior design projects to visualization projects as well as construction supervision and project management.

We deal with a variety of projects; Commercial, Residential, Offices, Stalls as well as pure 3D visualization projects providing our clients with accurate 3D models, designs and renders that show the actual completed look of the project.

WHY VALERIA VILLAS?

Luxurious Living Spaces

Each maisonette offers spacious and elegantly designed living areas, perfect for families or individuals seeking comfort and style. With 4 bedrooms, there's ample space for relaxation and entertainment, ensuring every member of the household has their own private retreat.

High-Quality Construction

Built with precision and attention to detail, these maisonettes showcase high-quality craftsmanship and premium materials throughout. From the foundation to the finishing touches, every aspect of the construction is designed to withstand the test of time.

Prime Location

Situated in a desirable location, the estate offers convenience and accessibility to key amenities such as schools, shopping centers, restaurants, and recreational facilities. Whether clients are commuting to work or exploring the local area, everything they need is just a stone's throw away.

Privacy and Security

The estate provides a secure and private environment for residents, with gated access and surveillance systems ensuring peace of mind at all times. Families can relax knowing that their safety and privacy are top priorities within the community.

Customization Options

Depending on the stage of construction, buyers may have the opportunity to customize certain features of their maisonette, allowing them to personalize their living space and create a home that truly reflects their unique style and preferences.

Detached Servants' Quarters

For clients who value privacy and independence, the estate offers the added convenience of a detached servants quarters. This separate living space provides accommodation for household staff or guests, ensuring that residents can enjoy their main residence without compromising on their privacy or comfort.

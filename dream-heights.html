DREAM HEIGHTS RESIDENCES

Dream Heights Residences is an residential development contracted to us by Kara Group Limited. The development comprises of 5 apartment blocks, a children's play area and a pool.

There are 2 3-bedroom apartment blocks, and 3 2-bedroom apartment blocks. There is ample parking of at least 1 car per house and additional parking for guests on the ground floor. The project has not yet begun construction but the design process is complete.

Year : 2021 & 2022

Company : Kara Group Limited.

Project Name : Dream Heights Residences.

Location : Oloolua, Ngong.
